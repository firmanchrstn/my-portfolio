Concept & Strategy

This poster design showcases the most tangible application of “Small Notes, Big Impact,” which seeks to use design to promote positive social behavior. Drawing direct inspiration from the aesthetics of movie posters, this poster narratively depicts the interactive engagement between team members and the university’s surrounding community, involving people such as online ride-hailing passengers and support staff.
